Welcome to Egg Hunter. This site was built on the idea of making NewEgg shopping easier and less complicated. When shopping on NewEgg, nothing takes more time than deciding which item in your search is the best one. Here, you are able to filter, rank, and view the items in many different ways hopefully helping to save time and money.
How?

Egg Hunter categorizes items based on individual ranks. The value for these ranks can be seen for each item. Addtionally, Egg Hunter allows you to sort items based off of a single rank, or multiple! Giving you lots of control.
